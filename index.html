Our Services

Pastry & Baking

Indulge in the sweet artistry of our pastry creations. Our expert pastry chefs craft delectable pastries, cakes, and confections that are not only visually stunning but also bursting with flavor. Perfect for celebrations or as a treat for yourself, our pastries are made with the finest ingredients and meticulous attention to detail.

Catering Services

Our catering services are crafted to bring both Nigerian and international cuisines to your events. Whether you're hosting a wedding, corporate event, or private gathering, our team of skilled chefs creates exquisite dishes that reflect the richness of Nigerian traditions and global flavors. From traditional Nigerian dishes like Jollof Rice and Egusi Soup to a variety of international options, we ensure that every meal is a culinary delight.

Bead Making

Discover the beauty and elegance of handcrafted bead jewelry. At Akiubaid Artisan Elegance, we design and create unique beadwork that reflects your personal style. Whether you're looking for a statement piece or a delicate accessory, our custom bead jewelry is perfect for adding a touch of sophistication to any outfit.

Plaiting Services

Transform your look with our professional hair styling services. Our talented stylists are dedicated to creating hairstyles that enhance your natural beauty and suit your individual preferences. From elegant updos for special occasions to trendy cuts and color treatments, we offer a range of services to keep you looking and feeling fabulous.
